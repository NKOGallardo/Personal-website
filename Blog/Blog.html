Why I Love Coding

Published on August 29, 2025 • 5 min read

Coding is more than just writing instructions for a computer — it's a way of thinking, solving problems, and building things that matter. I started coding as a hobby, and it quickly turned into my passion.

My Journey

It all began when I watched a simple YouTube tutorial on HTML and CSS. Within hours, I had built my first website. That thrill of creating something from scratch kept me hooked.

Why You Should Try Coding

  • It boosts problem-solving skills.

  • It's one of the most in-demand skills today.

If you're thinking about learning to code, start today. The journey might be tough, but it's worth it.
